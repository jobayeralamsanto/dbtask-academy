About this Course

The Android platform is gaining traction among developers and is a major player in the worldwide smartphone market.

Android development skills are likely to remain in high demand as more firms develop mobile apps for client involvement.

Android development is an useful skill that can help you advance your career, with over 2.5 billion active Android users worldwide. Android programming talents are in high demand globally, with companies spanning a wide range of industries.

The Android development tools you use will be determined by how you choose to focus your Android developer career.If you want to focus on developing native Android apps, you need be familiar with one or more of the following programming languages: Java, Kotlin.

A number of firms that produce native apps use Java and Kotlin.

What Will I Learn?

  • You can build any Android app you can think of. No matter if it is an idea that you or your friends have, or if it is a contract job that you need to develop.
  • You will build Apps for your portfolio to apply for Junior Android developer Jobs.
  • You'll be able to work as an Android freelancer and work from anywere in the world.
  • Publish your apps on Google Play and generate revenue with Google Pay and Google Ads
  • You'll be able to develop cloud apps using Google Firebase
  • You will be very confident using Kotlin and programming in general
